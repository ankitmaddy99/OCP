What is Beta Trial?

Your daily phone.
Our shared mission.

The Beta Trial means using the beta version of an OPPO device as your primary daily phone — reporting abnormal conditions and offering improvement suggestions as you go.

By joining, you become one of the first participants for OPPO's latest products, co-working directly with global engineers alongside a worldwide community of testers.

Rewards system

Three ways to earn
points & awards

Points convert directly into awards. The more you contribute, the richer your rewards will be.

1

Find more issues

Issue severity is converted into scores. Every valid bug report earns you points that exchange for awards — more bugs = more points.

2

Complete tasks actively

Fill questionnaires, complete security training, help recycle prototypes, or recruit friends to the Beta Club — each task pays in points.

3

Join activities positively

Special project activities are set up during trial periods. Complete them to earn bonus scores on top of your regular contributions.

Annual recognition

The OCP Award

The OCP Awards recognise top beta hunters based on yearly accumulated points. Past winners have received exclusive kits, merchandise, and global recognition across the OPPO community.

Note: OCP Awards are based on yearly accumulated points and positions. Award items and requirements may change based on management decisions.

Exclusive OCP Award Kit

Top performers receive the official Pioneer Award kit — a recognised token for shaping OPPO innovation.

Gift cards & merchandise

Points convert to gift cards, branded merchandise, and exclusive OPPO Community rewards.

Global event invitations
